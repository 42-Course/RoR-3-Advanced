Background "stamp" art
======================

Drop transparent PNGs here to have them appear as faint, fixed background
decorations behind the whole app. The layout reserves two slots:

  stamp-1.png   -> top-right corner
  stamp-2.png   -> bottom-left corner

They are rendered by the `.stamp-field` rule in
app/assets/stylesheets/application.css at ~6% opacity, so use bold, simple,
high-contrast shapes (line art / seals / monograms work best).

No PNGs here yet? The app looks fine — the slots simply stay empty.
